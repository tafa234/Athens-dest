Vacations in Athens

350$ - 3 Days

"Experience the essence of Athens in just two days, immersing yourself in its rich history and vibrant culture."

Begin your journey with a visit to the iconic Acropolis, where the Parthenon stands as a testament to ancient Greek architecture and offers stunning views of the city. Wander through the historic Plaka neighborhood, filled with charming shops and traditional tavernas. In the afternoon, explore the ancient Agora, the center of Athenian democracy, and the nearby Roman Agora. On the second day, delve into the treasures of the National Archaeological Museum, home to an impressive collection of Greek antiquities. End your trip with a visit to the picturesque Cape Sounion, where the Temple of Poseidon overlooks the sparkling Aegean Sea, offering a perfect sunset view. Throughout your stay, indulge in authentic Greek cuisine, from savory souvlaki to sweet baklava, making your short visit to Athens truly unforgettable."

Day 1: Athens' Ancient Landmarks

- Visit the Acropolis and Parthenon to enjoy panoramic views of Athens.
- Explore the historic Plaka neighborhood and have lunch at a traditional taverna.
- Discover Monastiraki Square and its lively market, and enjoy dinner at a restaurant with a view of the Acropolis.

Day 2: Museums and Modern Athens

- Visit the National Archaeological Museum to explore Greek antiquities.
- Visit the Roman Agora and Hadrian's Library, and have lunch in the trendy Psiri district.
- Watch the sunset from Mount Lycabettus, and explore the nightlife in the Gazi district.

Day 3: Coastal Excursions

- Visit the Benaki Museum or the Museum of Cycladic Art.
- Visit Syntagma Square to watch the changing of the guard and stroll through the National Garden.
- Take a trip to Cape Sounion to visit the Temple of Poseidon and watch the sunset, then return to Athens for dinner.
